Hen's chili

The aji de gallina recipe is another of the great wonders of Peruvian food.

It has a mixture of interesting ingredients that give it a unique, extremely delicious flavor. Its appearance is that of a creamy dish, similar to a stew, yellow in color.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole breast of chicken with bone
  • 3 Peruvian yellow chili peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 4 walnut halves
  • 2 packages of soda crackers or 2 slices of sliced bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 black olives
  • 1 boiled egg

Steps

  1. -Place the water in a large pot, bring to a boil and then add the sachet of Gourmet Powdered Chicken Bouillon Powder and stir until it dissolves. Add the whole chicken breast and cook for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
  2. Remove chicken from broth and shred with hands. Separately set aside the chicken and the broth in which it was cooked.
  3. Heat the oil in a large pot and cook the onion with the chili, season with Gourmet Sea Salt and Gourmet Pepper Mix. Cook until onion is translucent and soft.
  4. Soak the bread in a cup of the leftover chicken broth. Drain the bread and then blend with the evaporated milk, onion and chili peppers. Return everything to the pot and cook for 8 minutes. Add Gourmet Ground Turmeric, shredded chicken, Parmesan cheese and walnuts. If it is too thick, add the reserved chicken broth a little at a time
  5. Serve with white rice, a quartered hard-boiled egg and olives.
