Stir Fried Loin

Is a popular fusion dish that blends Chinese stir-fry traditions with Peruvian ingredients including aji amarillo, cilantro and tomato.

This hearty main dish is usually accompanied by fluffy white rice and french fries. You can feel free to combine this Peruvian Lomo Saltado recipe with your favorite side dishes to create your own cultural fusions at the table. Learn how to make Lomo Saltado now with this recipe and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups frozen French fries
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small red onion, thickly sliced
  • 2 roma tomatoes, thickly sliced
  • 2 2heaping tablespoons aji amarillo chile paste

Steps

  1. Season the meat with garlic, salt and pepper.
  2. Cook white rice according to package directions.
  3. Prepare fries according to package directions.
  4. Meanwhile, heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add oil and brown meat, a few slices at a time (so they don't steam and the meat browns). Cook each batch for 2 minutes.
  5. Add onion, tomato and chili paste; cook and stir for 2 to 3 minutes. The tomatoes and onions should be crisp, not mushy. Add the soy sauce and vinegar pouring down the sides of the wok or skillet; mix well. Add beef and beef broth; cook 1 to 2 minutes or until heated through. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with chopped cilantro. Serve with rice and french fries.
